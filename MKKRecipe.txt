<h1>Brazillian Style Collard Greens<img src="../static/BrazilFlag.png" style="width:50px;height:30px;"></h1>
<br/>
<h2>Ingredients:</h2>
<ul>
    <li>1 clove of garlic</li>
    <li>1 tbsp olive oil</li>
    <li>1 tsp pepper (light or regular)</li>
    <li>1 lb collard greens</li>
    <li>1 tsp salt</li>
</ul>
<h2>Directions:</h2>

<h3>Preparation:</h3>
<p>Chop up the garlic clove.</p>
<p>Rinse the greens well with cold water.</p>
<p>Pile several leaves on top of each other. Roll the leaves up together length wise.</p>
<p>With a sharp knife, slice the roll of leaves into thin strips.</p>
<h3>Cooking:</h3>
<p>Heat the oil in a large frying pan</p>
<p>Saute the chopped garlic.</p>
<p>Add the greens and saute.</p>
<p>Add salt to taste.</p>
<p>Serve immediately.</p>
<h4>Serves 4</h4>
